Project Idea

Overview: I’m interested in developing a project idea of an app which feels like a personal stylist in your pocket. The app uses an algorithm to create a combination of outfits to wear by using images straight from peoples closets by providing a photo of their t-shirts, shoes, accessories etc, from their camera roll or camera to the app. There will be specific styles/aesthetics/occasions/tags you can input into the app to create a fit for you by recognising certain features and colours from the images of your own uploads (the clothes), and matching it with the associated keywords/ you inputted. This will finally create an output of a column of images from head to toe

Motivation: This project may be lightly useful for people who struggle to decide what to wear and spend a good 20 minutes searching for something right and suitable to wear out/in. Appearance may bring confidence to others who are struggling with assurance towards themselves. Therefore by having a system that specifically tackles that problem, may reduce the anxiety of not finding the right thing to wear, the time it takes to choose and the lack of confidence if you don't feel comfortable with what you're wearing. It's also interesting as it may bring about new combinations you haven't thought of yourself and just having fun with the app itself.

Description: Initially the app loads you into the service in which the user may customize their profile and enter in details regarding the styles they are interested in (e.g selecting tags like casual, gothic, cute, street) and makes it the default keyword when generating outfit combinations. However obviously each time you generate a fit you can select different keywords to fit the occasion, the initial tags are just for efficiency and convenience. Within the app you can select multiple photos from the photo library of any clothes/accessories/shoes you take a photo of or find images of and load it into the app's database/own library. The more imagery you select the greater the variety of custom fits. When loading in images from your photo library the system may detect similar/the same clothes that they have found a professional/cleaner image (with white background or from a store image) of and asks if the user would like to replace it for an improved experience.

You are then taken into the ‘styling’ screen where you select tags if needed and input such as- weather (the current weather will already be loaded in with location permissions on), colours, if you want to style with a certain piece of clothing etc, that you want each for each generated fit. You can then initialize the process where a small panel (a page like setting, however app friendly) is created of columns of different pieces of clothing from head to toe that is complimented together with users needs. A description of the imputed related details will also be presented on the panel. There is a button in which the user can constantly press to continue generating more pieces until one that interests them or they can select/unselect one-multiple pieces of clothing the user would like to leave in/out the generated collection. A side by side comparison of the person wearing the outfit can also be posted/saved into the generated column of each liked fit in their library. When they find a piece they like, the user has a choice of liking the generated outfit and saving it to the app library on their profile.

There is an option to make your collections public or private to be able to share their experience with their friends or connect from users around the globe based on a friending system within the app. If the profile is made public or that specific panel, then different users can search any keywords relating to clothes in any way to find inspiration from others who are like minded, and have the option to save different panels for their own use.

Tools and Technologies: As this project will most likely be an app, a platform/software to build the app such as Appery.io, Mobile Roadie etc will be appropriate to utilize. A substantial aspect in this project will be for the app to perform object and feature detection through automated image recognition implemented into the program from the input of clothes in the uploaded photo library. As there is a need to make sense for associated keywords/imagery for the app to be able to achieve its purpose. For images with a convoluted background, the app will binarize the image to outline specific structural features to work with. Machine learning may also be a significant factor of the app as it gains more data from users’ feedback, the program will learn to execute better output.

Skills Required: The skills needed in this project is most likely to be able to write in a few coding languages. Java would mostly be useful as applications generally use that language. Being able to design the interface is also another skill needed to present this app in an aesthetic manner that captivates users and encourages the use of it. The hardware needed is a computer for the initial code but also ios/android platforms to be able to test the app. The idea itself would be feasible however there may be complex components towards coding and implementing specific image recognition software as well as machine learning, therefore an experienced coder/developer may need to work on this project to reach the desired outcome.

Outcome: If the project were to be successful then there’ll be people who will be appreciative and have reduced anxiety towards figuring out what to wear if that action brings stress towards their daily lives. Creating this project will also be a major accomplishment for a platform due to its complex implementations of different mechanics/code. Furthermore it’ll continue pushing the boundaries of what developers can achieve. In light of this it may also intrigue people and allow them to play around with the mechanisms of the app itself. This allows for others to be more confident and own their style through interactions with friends as well as a subliminal boost from knowing that an AI has matched it to be a perfect fit for the user.
